# Google OAuth Authentication & PostHog Analytics

This release introduces a complete Google OAuth 2.0 authentication system, enabling seamless user onboarding and detailed analytics of user behavior.

You can now sign in or register using your Google account:

- **One-Click Authentication**: No need to remember another password - just use your Google account
- **Choose Your Username**: New users can pick their preferred username after signing in with Google
- **Instant Access**: No email verification needed - start using Cylink immediately
- **Secure & Safe**: Your Google credentials are never shared with us

## How to Use

### For New Users

1. Click "Continue with Google" on the registration page
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Choose your preferred username
4. Start creating short links immediately!

### For Existing Users

1. Click "Continue with Google" on the login page
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Access your dashboard instantly
