# Your Voice Matters: Introducing the Feedback Board

Ever wished you could tell us what to build next? Now you can! The **Feedback Board** is your new space to share ideas and help shape CyLink's future.

## What You Can Do

- **Submit Feature Requests** — Tell us what new features you'd love to see
- **Report Bugs** — Help us squash issues you've encountered
- **Vote on Ideas** — Upvote what you want, downvote what doesn't fit
- **Find Similar Ideas** — Search and discover what others have suggested
- **See Supporters** — Check who's backing each idea

## Get Started

1. Head to your **Dashboard**
2. Click on **Feedback** in the menu
3. Hit **New Feedback** and share your thoughts!

Your vote counts — popular ideas are more likely to become reality.
